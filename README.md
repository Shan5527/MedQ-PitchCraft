# 🌍 Problem

Healthcare systems often suffer from:

* ❌ Long hospital queues
* ❌ Poor triage before hospital visits
* ❌ No visibility of bed availability
* ❌ Patients visiting wrong departments
* ❌ Lack of quick medical guidance

Patients frequently arrive at hospitals **without knowing urgency**, creating overcrowded OPDs and inefficient care delivery.

---

# 💡 Our Solution — MediFlow

**MediFlow** is an intelligent healthcare assistance platform that helps patients:

✔ Understand symptoms using AI
✔ Find the right hospital quickly
✔ Track queue and waiting times
✔ Discover available beds
✔ Talk to medical professionals instantly

It acts as a **smart bridge between patients and hospitals**.

---

# 🚀 Core Features

## 🤖 AI Symptom Checker

Guided AI-based health triage that asks intelligent follow-up questions to determine urgency.

```
User symptoms
      ↓
AI follow-up questions
      ↓
Urgency classification
      ↓
Hospital recommendation
```

---

## 🧠 Mental Health Screening

A dedicated mental wellness check that evaluates stress, anxiety, and emotional wellbeing.

Features:

* Conversational screening
* Short questionnaire
* Wellness recommendations
* Option to connect with a professional

---

## 👨‍⚕️ Talk to Medical Expert

Instant access to real healthcare professionals for quick advice.

```
Patient Concern
      ↓
AI Symptom Check
      ↓
Connect to Doctor / Nurse
      ↓
Chat or Voice Consultation
```

Patients receive **guidance before visiting hospitals**, reducing unnecessary crowding.

---

## 🏥 Hospital Queue Tracking

Patients can see:

* Current queue number
* Estimated waiting time
* Department availability

This allows users to **plan visits intelligently**.

---

## 🛏 Bed Availability System

Hospitals update real-time bed availability.

Patients can instantly find:

* ICU beds
* Emergency beds
* General ward beds

---
