## 📖 Overview
MedQ isn't just another medical app. It’s a **multi-layered spatial experience**. We’ve ditched flat, boring interfaces for a tactile "Paper Stack" design that makes navigation intuitive for users of all ages.

### 🌟 Key Pillars
- **Physicality:** Every card has a weight and a shadow.
- **Clarity:** High-contrast text and oversized buttons.
- **Speed:** Zero-scroll-barrier architecture.

## 📐 Design System: "Soft UI"
We prioritize **Universal Design**. Here is how we achieve depth without visual fatigue:

> [!TIP]
> **Why No Glassmorphism?** > While glass effects look modern, they often fail accessibility tests for low-vision users. MedQ uses **Solid Elevation** instead.

* **Background:** `#F4F7F9` (Soft off-white to reduce eye strain).
* **Surface:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure white cards for high contrast).
* **Elevation:** Multi-layered `0 10px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)` shadows.
* **Radius:** `24px` for a friendly, approachable feel.
