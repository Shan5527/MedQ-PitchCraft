# **App Name**: MediW

## Core Features:

- AI Symptom Triage: An AI-powered tool that analyzes reported symptoms to recommend appropriate hospital departments, urgency levels, and includes an emergency fast lane alert for critical conditions.
- Digital Queue Token: Displays the patient's personal queue token, department, assigned doctor, number of patients ahead, and estimated wait time.
- Real-time Live Queue Tracker: Provides a live view of the hospital's current serving token, next upcoming tokens, and highlights the user's position in the queue with smart wait time prediction.
- Real-time Bed Availability: Shows real-time availability of different bed types (e.g., ICU, Oxygen) across nearby hospitals with clear status indicators.
- Transparent Billing Explainer: Breaks down hospital bills into itemized costs, provides explanations for each charge, and compares prices against average market costs.
- Patient Visit History: Allows patients to review their past medical visits, including date, doctor, diagnosis, and prescription details.
- One-click Queue Join: Enables patients to quickly join a hospital queue directly from the home dashboard or after AI symptom triage.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Trustworthy and professional medium-dark blue (#2e62bb). Used for major CTAs, active states, and branding elements.
- Background Color: A clean, desaturated cool white with a subtle hint of the primary blue hue (#eceef2). Ensures clarity and visual calmness.
- Accent Color: A clear and fresh health-green (#3aa75f) explicitly used for health status indicators, positive affirmations, and 'available' states.
- Body and Headline Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable characteristics, suitable for conveying critical healthcare information clearly.
- Minimal icons: Clean, simple line icons that enhance usability without overwhelming the interface, consistent with a professional healthcare aesthetic.
- Mobile-first card-based layout with rounded corners and generous spacing. Key actions are highlighted with large, prominent buttons to ensure easy interaction and navigation.
- Subtle, smooth transitions and micro-interactions for state changes, navigation, and feedback, providing a fluid and modern user experience without distraction.
